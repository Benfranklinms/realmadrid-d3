Jude Bellingham El Clasico

El Clasico for the ages saw Jude Bellingham etch his name in football history, as the English midfielder delivered two sensational goals, including a dramatic stoppage-time winner, to spearhead Real Madrid to a thrilling 2-1 comeback triumph over Barcelona in the Spanish league on Saturday. Bellingham's first Clasico appearance turned into a fairy-tale story, as his electrifying performance led Real Madrid to victory, marking his 12th and 13th goals in just 13 games across all competitions since his summer transfer to the Spanish giants.
